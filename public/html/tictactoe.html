Tic-Tac-Toe Game

RULES

  1. The Game is Played on 3*3 matrix (i.e 3 Squares by 3 Squares )

  2. You are X , and Computer is O .

  3. Player take turns putting their marks in empty squares.

  4. The first player to get 3 of his marks in a top , down ,across or diagonal is the winner .

  5. When all the 9 square are full , the game is over. If no player has 3 marks in a row then the game is tie .
