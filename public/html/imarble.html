Imarble Game

RULES

  1. First identify a hole that is followed by 2 consecutive marbles either verically or horizontally, left or right . A hole refers to a position where the marble is absent.
  2. To remove the marble from the board by jumping an adjacent marble over it into a hole .
  3. Jumping is done by moving horizontally or vertically . Diagonal moves are not allowed.
  4. Continue these steps to remove marble one-by-one till only one is left on the board.
  5. Consecutive moves with the same marble is called a sweep and is counted as one move.
  6. Try to solve the board in minimum no. of moves to get a higher score.
  7. Click on Start to proceed .
